WELCOME!

Hello and welcome to my page. I'm a researcher at UC Irvine working with Twitter and disaster data on Project HEROIC. Using statistical methods, I tackle questions regarding message retransmission and follower behavior. I write and implement my own scripts to assist our lab in cleaning, modeling and visualizing data.

I am also an R Instructor with UCI's Data Science Initiative where I have the satisfaction of passing along tips, tricks, and techniques to future data scientists and researchers in the community. My areas of interest primarily focus on applied statistics and machine learning and the ways in which they can help harness large amounts of data.

In my free time, I like to build productivity tools, interactive Shiny apps for data visualization, and web-scrapers for data acquisition and by night, I run a small event production company called EES. Feel free to explore the rest of my site and contact me if you have any questions!
